# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: March 3, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how Outstanding Manager ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect

### User Account Information
When you create an account, we collect your email address and display name.

### Business Data
The app allows you to store and manage business records, including:
*   Party names and contact details.
*   Invoice details (amounts, dates, items).
*   Payment records.

This data is stored securely in Firebase (Google Cloud).

## 2. How We Use Information
We use the information to:
*   Provide and maintain the application.
*   Synchronize your data across devices.
*   Generate business reports (PDF/Excel) as requested by you.

## 3. Data Storage and Security
Your data is stored using Firebase Firestore and protected by Firebase Authentication. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

## 4. Data Deletion
You can delete your account and all associated data at any time through the **Settings > Delete Account** option within the app.

## 5. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
