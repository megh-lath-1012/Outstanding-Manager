# Account Deletion Guide

At Outstanding Manager, we believe in giving you full control over your data. You can delete your account and all associated information at any time.

## How to Delete Your Account

1.  Open the **Outstanding Manager** app.
2.  Navigate to the **Settings** tab (from the bottom navigation bar).
3.  Scroll down to the **ACCOUNT** section.
4.  Tap on **Delete Account**.
5.  A confirmation dialog will appear warning you that this action is permanent.
6.  Confirm the deletion.

## What Happens When You Delete Your Account?

*   **Authentication**: Your login credentials will be removed from our authentication system.
*   **Business Records**: All your Parties, Invoices, and Payment records will be permanently deleted from our database.
*   **Profile**: Your business profile and settings will be removed.
*   **Irreversibility**: This action cannot be undone. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered.

## Data Retention
We do not retain any personal or business data after the account deletion process is complete.
