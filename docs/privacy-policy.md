# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: March 2026**

Welcome to the Outstanding Management App ("App"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and handle your information when you use our App.

## 1. Information We Collect

### A. Account Information
When you create an account, we may collect your email address, display name, and optional company details (such as company name, logo, GST number, and address) to personalize your app experience.

### B. Business Data
The core functionality of the App is to manage your financial ledger. We securely store the data you enter, including details about your customers/suppliers ("Parties"), Invoices, Payments, and Cashflow projections. 

### C. Automatically Collected Data
We may collect basic usage data (like crash reports and app interaction metrics) to help us improve the app's stability and performance.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect strictly to:
- Provide, operate, and maintain the App's core ledger and forecasting features.
- Authenticate you upon login securely.
- Generate smart insights (via AI) on your cashflow and invoice data.

## 3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using **Google Cloud Firestore**. 
- **Security Rules**: Your data is isolated. Only you—when fully authenticated into your specific account—can access or modify your business data and subcollections.
- **Authentication**: We use Firebase Authentication to ensure industry-standard security practices for your account access.

## 4. Third-Party Services (AI)

The App utilizes an integrated AI Assistant ("Pulse") powered by Google's Gemini API to help you record transactions via natural language and provide cashflow forecasting. When you use these specific AI features, the text fragments you input or the invoice summaries analyzed may be processed by these external models to generate responses.

## 5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact the developer through our app store listing or the GitHub repository issues page.
